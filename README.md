## 🔥 Core Features

### 1. Extreme CPU & GPU Overclocking

Stock BBK kernels often heavily restrict the CPU and GPU to favor battery life and lower surface temperatures. This app allows you to force your prime and performance cores to run at their absolute maximum clock speeds.

* **Locked Max Frequencies:** Prevents the system from scaling down the CPU during heavy gaming sessions.
* **GPU Boost:** Forces the GPU to its highest power state for maximum FPS in demanding titles.
* *Note: This module focuses purely on pushing maximum power. **Undervolting is NOT supported** in this release.*

### 2. Complete Thermal Limit Removal

BBK devices are notoriously aggressive with thermal throttling, automatically dimming your screen or cutting your frame rate in half the moment the phone gets warm.

* **Disable Thermal Engine:** This app kills the stock thermal daemons tracking your battery and CPU temperatures.
* **No More Frame Drops:** Your device will run at maximum frequencies regardless of how hot it gets.
* *Warning: Your phone will get extremely hot. It is highly recommended to use a snap-on thermoelectric phone cooler when this feature is active.*

### 3. Native Bypass Charging

Prolong your battery's lifespan and drastically reduce heat while gaming while plugged in.

* **Direct Motherboard Power:** When activated, the APK triggers the hidden bypass charging nodes in ColorOS/RealmeUI/OxygenOS. The power from your charging cable goes directly to the phone's hardware, completely bypassing the battery.
* **Zero Charge Cycle Wear:** The battery percentage stays locked where it is, generating zero charging heat and saving you from unnecessary battery degradation.

### 4. Fullscreen Always-On Display (AOD)

Break free from the restrictive.

* **Edge-to-Edge Canvas:** Overrides the system UI limitations to allow the Always-On Display to utilize the entire screen.
* **No Timeout:** Prevents the system from aggressively shifting or dimming the AOD, keeping your custom visuals front and center at all times.

## 🛠️ Installation & Setup

1. **Download the APK:** Grab the latest `.apk` file from the Releases section.
2. **Install:** Install the app normally on your device.
3. **Grant Root:** Open the app. You will immediately be prompted by Magisk to grant **Superuser Request**. You MUST select "Grant".
4. **Apply Tweaks:** Navigate through the app dashboard to toggle Bypass Charging, select your Animation Level (1-4), and enable Overclocking/Thermal removal.
5. **Reboot:** A system reboot is recommended after applying AOD or Animation changes to ensure the framework registers the new values.
